Login Page

This page allows students to log in to the ERP system using their registered credentials.

Note: Login using:
  • Username: Student Login
  • Password: Application Code / Password

Student Dashboard

After successful login, the dashboard provides access to academic services.

Action: Click on the "Academics cum Examination Enrollment" button to proceed.

Student Enrollment Form

Complete academic enrollment by selecting subjects and providing required info.

Note: Choose subjects per eligibility, fill details carefully, and click Submit.

Student Form Final View

Review the final preview of the enrollment form before payment.

Note: Select the "Declaration" checkbox to confirm accuracy before proceeding to payment.

Payment Page

Complete the enrollment process by paying the required fees.

Options: Pay using UPI, Debit/Credit Card, or Net Banking.

Payment Receipt Page

Generate and download receipt for future reference.

Success: Click the Print button to download the receipt.

Login as Principal / HOD

Authorized login for Principal or Head of Department (HOD).

Principal Dashboard

Administrative dashboard for approvals.

Navigation: Go to "Enrollment" section and click "Approve Student".

Enrollment Search & Filters

Filter student enrollment records for review.

Note: Select course category, discipline, academic session, and semester from dropdowns.

Student Enrollment List

Review list of pending student enrollments.

Available Actions:
  • Approve: Click "Approve" to validate enrollment.
  • Reject (Attendance): Click "Reject due to Low Attendance" if applicable.
  • Reject (Fees): Click "Reject due to Fees Not Paid" if applicable.

Login as a Student

This page allows students to log in to the ERP system in order to access examination-related services.

Student Dashboard

After successful login, the student is redirected to the dashboard where examination-related documents are available.

Note: From the dashboard, the student can download the admit card by clicking the "Download Admit Card" button.
